Capabilities

Everything runs on your machine

A full ML pipeline — transcription, speaker diarization, voice matching, and structured note generation — running locally on your GPU.

Background Recording

A lightweight Android app records meetings in the background with the screen off. Foreground Service keeps capture alive and reliable. Nobody knows you're recording.

WiFi Auto‑Upload

One tap after the meeting ends. The recording transfers to your laptop over local WiFi in seconds. QR code pairing — zero manual configuration.

Local ML Pipeline

faster‑whisper (large‑v3) for Hinglish transcription, pyannote for speaker diarization, resemblyzer for voice embeddings — all GPU‑accelerated on your RTX.

Speaker Recognition

Name each voice once — Arc remembers forever. Voice embeddings match speakers across sessions automatically. Gemma infers names from conversation context.

Obsidian Notes

Structured markdown with YAML frontmatter, summaries, decisions, action items, and [[wikilinks]] — written directly to your vault. Query with Claude via MCP.

Zero Cloud

No data leaves your machine. No cloud APIs. No subscriptions. No telemetry. Your conversations stay yours — permanently and completely.
